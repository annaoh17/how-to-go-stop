How to Go-Stop! Learn the Cards! How to Play!

3 People!

-Click to read instructions!-

  • First, select who the dealer will be.
  • After choosing the dealer, the dealer will shuffle the cards.
  • After shuffling, the dealer asks their opponent to “cut” the deck—this means taking off a portion of the cards and placing them in the middle of the deck.
  • With the remaining stack of cards, the dealer places 3 cards face up around the deck.
  • Then they will deal 3 cards to their opponent first, and then 5 cards to themselves.
  • The dealer places 3 more cards face up around the deck.
  • The dealer deals another 4 cards to their opponent and then to themselves.
  • After dealing, the dealer takes the remaining stack and places it onto the central deck, which is surrounded by 6 cards.
  • Each player should have 8 cards in their hand.
